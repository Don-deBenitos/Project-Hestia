Images for the site (place files in this folder):

  banner.jpg   — Homepage hero background (top section)
  hestia.jpg   — Homepage carousel cards + Gallery page photos

Your original may be in the project's  image  folder as  IMG_5384.HEIC.
Browsers cannot display HEIC; convert to JPG or PNG first.

To convert HEIC to JPG:
- Windows: Open in Photos app → export/save as JPG.
- Or use a free HEIC-to-JPG converter online, then save here as  banner.jpg  and/or  hestia.jpg .
